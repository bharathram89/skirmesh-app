Player Section
Find or bring fields running the Skrimish system.

1) Register for a profile

2) Buy an RFID wristband

3) Connect RFID wristband to profile

4) Play games at Skrimish enabled fields to collects stats

5) View and share stats

Field Section
Reach out to Skrimish to outfit your field The follow below steps.

1) Turn On devices

2) Configure Game Modes in your profile

3) Start Game

4) Manage game

5) View and share stats
